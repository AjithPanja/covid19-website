About The Corona Virus

Coronaviruses are a group of related viruses in mammals and birds causing respiratory tract infections. It is derived from the Latin word corona, meaning "crown", which refers to the characteristic appearance of a crown or a solar corona around the virus particles, due to the surface being covered in club-shaped protein spikes.The first corona virus was discovered in the 1930s of domesticated chickens. Human coronaviruses were discovered in the 1960s. These viruses can cause lethal diseases such as SARS, MERS, and COVID-19.

Precautions

1. The virus enters your body via your eyes, nose and/or mouth, so it is important to avoid touching your face with unwashed hands.

2. There is no evidence that face masks will effectively prevent you from becoming infected with the virus.

3. Wash your hands with soap and water for at least 20 seconds.

4.Do not unnecessarily touch any surfaces or objects as they could ne contaminated.
